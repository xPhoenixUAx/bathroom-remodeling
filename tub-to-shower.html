Tub-to-Shower Conversion

A tub-to-shower conversion updates your bathroom for safety, accessibility, and style. We remove the existing tub, prepare substrate, install an acrylic or tile shower base, and finish with premium fixtures.

Benefits & Options

  • Improved accessibility and safety
  • Modern glass enclosures and low-threshold bases
  • Durable acrylic walls or tile options

Process & Timeline

Most conversions complete in 1–3 days depending on materials and site conditions. We provide a clear timeline during the free consultation.
