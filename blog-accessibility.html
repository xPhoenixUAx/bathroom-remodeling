Designing for Accessibility: Walk-In Tubs & Curbless Showers

Nov 12, 2025 • Accessibility

Accessible bathrooms balance safety with an uncluttered aesthetic. Consider non-slip floors, correctly positioned grab bars, integrated seating, and generous clearances for mobility devices. Curbless showers paired with proper drainage and waterproofing offer both style and access.

Work with your installer to meet ADA guidelines where needed and to tailor solutions that fit your home's layout.
