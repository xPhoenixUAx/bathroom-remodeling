5 Ideas to Modernize Your Bathroom

Jan 6, 2026 • Design

Updating a bathroom doesn't always mean a full renovation. Here are five approachable ideas that deliver a modern look and improved function:

  1. Statement Tile: Use a bold tile on a feature wall or as an accent floor to add personality.
  2. Streamlined Fixtures: Swap dated faucets and showerheads for slim, matte-finish fixtures to modernize hardware.
  3. Layered Lighting: Add task, ambient, and accent lighting for a flexible and luxurious feel.
  4. Smart Storage: Reconfigure vanity storage with pull-outs and hidden compartments to reduce clutter.
  5. Neutral Palette with Texture: Use a calm color base with textured materials (wood, stone, or matte tiles) for depth.
