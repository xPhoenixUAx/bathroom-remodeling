Maintaining Acrylic Walls and Liners

Dec 18, 2025 • Maintenance

Acrylic walls and liners are low-maintenance but benefit from consistent care. Use non-abrasive cleaners, avoid bleach-based concentrates, and rinse after use. For stubborn stains, a paste of baking soda and water applied gently can help.

Check seals and caulking annually and address chips early to prevent moisture issues. For heavy wear, consult your installer about reconditioning or warranty options.
