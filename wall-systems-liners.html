Acrylic Wall Systems & Shower Liners

Fast, watertight, and low-maintenance wall systems that refresh bathrooms with minimal disruption.

Get a Quick Estimate Learn About Options

Why choose acrylic walls?

Acrylic panels install over your existing surface, creating a seamless, non-porous barrier that resists mold and staining while offering a premium finish.

Waterproof Seal

Engineered joints and tight seams for lasting protection.

Low Maintenance

Non-porous finish wipes clean — no grout or tile upkeep.

Fast Install

Most installs completed in a single day — minimal downtime.

Typical installation steps

  1. Assess and prepare surface — remove fixtures as needed.
  2. Custom-fit acrylic panels and ensure waterproof junctions.
  3. Reinstall hardware and test seals; final quality walkthrough.

Frequently asked questions

Are acrylic walls durable?

Yes — high-quality acrylic panels resist cracking and discoloration when installed correctly.

Will the install reveal underlying damage?

If underlying rot or water damage is found, we will notify you and provide repair options before completing the install.
