About LuxuryBath Studio

Founded in 2010, LuxuryBath Studio began as a small team of craftsmen and designers dedicated to turning bathrooms into safer, more beautiful spaces. Today we operate nationally, combining in‑house quality oversight with a vetted network of licensed contractors. Our mission is to deliver fast, reliable remodels with transparent pricing and a satisfaction warranty for homeowners across the United States.

Our Mission & Values

We focus on safety, longevity, and modern design. Whether fitting a walk-in tub for accessibility or installing premium acrylic wall systems, our vetted pros prioritize comfort and code-compliant workmanship.
